## Online Courses

### ROS 2 & Robotics Fundamentals
1. **The Construct** - ROS 2 for Beginners (Free + Paid tiers)
   - URL: https://www.theconstructsim.com/
   - Focus: Hands-on ROS 2 Humble, Gazebo, Nav2
   - Time: 40 hours

2. **Udemy** - ROS 2 for Beginners (by Edouard Renard)
   - Focus: Nodes, topics, services, actions, launch files
   - Time: 10 hours
   - Cost: ~$15 (frequent sales)

3. **ETH Zürich** - Programming for Robotics (YouTube, Free)
   - URL: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLE-BQwvVGf8HOvwXPgtDfWoxd4Cc6ghiP
   - Focus: ROS 1 basics (concepts transfer to ROS 2)
   - Time: 20 lectures

### Simulation & Digital Twins
4. **NVIDIA Isaac Sim Tutorials** (Official, Free)
   - URL: https://docs.omniverse.nvidia.com/isaacsim/latest/
   - Focus: Isaac Sim basics, ROS 2 bridge, RL training
   - Time: Self-paced

5. **Unity Learn** - Robotics Courses (Free)
   - URL: https://learn.unity.com/course/unity-robotics-hub
   - Focus: Unity Robotics Hub, ROS-TCP-Connector
   - Time: 6 hours

### Machine Learning & RL
6. **DeepMind x UCL** - Deep RL Course (YouTube, Free)
   - URL: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLqYmG7hTraZDVH599EItlEWsUOsJbAodm

   - Time: 18 lectures

7. **Hugging Face** - NLP & Transformers Course (Free)
   - URL: https://huggingface.co/learn/nlp-course/

## Books

### Robotics & ROS 2
1. **Programming Robots with ROS 2** - Morgan Quigley et al. (2024)
   - Publisher: O'Reilly (Est. release Q2 2025)
   - Focus: ROS 2 architecture, advanced patterns

2. **Modern Robotics** - Kevin Lynch & Frank Park (2017)
   - Free PDF: http://hades.mech.northwestern.edu/index.php/Modern_Robotics
   - Focus: Kinematics, dynamics, motion planning (theory)

### Machine Learning
3. **Deep Learning** - Ian Goodfellow et al. (2016)
   - Free: https://www.deeplearningbook.org/
   - Focus: ML foundations, CNNs, RNNs, optimization

4. **Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction** - Sutton & Barto (2nd ed., 2018)
   - Free PDF: http://incompleteideas.net/book/the-book-2nd.html
   - Focus: RL theory (MDPs, value functions, policy gradients)

## Datasets

### Robotics Manipulation
1. **Open X-Embodiment** (1M+ episodes, 22 robot platforms)
   - URL: https://robotics-transformer-x.github.io/
   - Use: VLA training, sim-to-real transfer
   - Size: ~500GB

2. **RoboNet** (15M frames, 7 robot arms)
   - URL: https://www.robonet.wiki/
   - Use: Imitation learning, video prediction
   - Size: ~150GB

### Navigation & SLAM
3. **KITTI Dataset** (Outdoor autonomous driving)
   - URL: http://www.cvlibs.net/datasets/kitti/
   - Use: Visual odometry, object detection
   - Size: ~100GB

4. **TUM RGB-D Dataset** (Indoor SLAM)
   - URL: https://vision.in.tum.de/data/datasets/rgbd-dataset
   - Use: Benchmarking VSLAM algorithms
   - Size: ~40GB

### Speech & Language
5. **LibriSpeech** (1000 hours clean English speech)
   - URL: https://www.openslr.org/12/
   - Use: Training custom speech models
   - Size: ~60GB

## Software Tools

- **GitHub Copilot**: AI pair programmer (paid, free for students)
